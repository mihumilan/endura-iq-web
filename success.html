Welcome to the Team! ⚡

Your payment was successful. Let's get you set up on the Endura IQ platform.

1 Create Your Account

Click the button below to open the Endura IQ App. Go to the "Sign Up" (Rejestracja) tab and create your unique username and password.

Open Endura IQ App

2 Install as an App (Mobile)

You don't need the App Store. To get the full, full-screen mobile experience:

  • iPhone (Safari): Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom, scroll down, and select "Add to Home Screen".
  • Android (Chrome): Tap the 3 dots in the top right corner and select "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen".

3 Complete Your Athlete Profile

Once you log in, the app will automatically ask you to fill out the Onboarding Survey. Please be honest about your fitness, injuries, and available training hours. I use this data to craft your first macrocycle.

4 Connect Garmin

To receive planned workouts directly on your watch and sync your completed activities, go to the "Athlete Data" tab in the menu, click on "Integrations 🔗", and enter your Garmin Connect email and password.

Got questions? Once you're inside the app, go to the Messages tab and say Hi! Let's get to work.
