Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection and Health Information (GDPR)

Endura IQ collects specific health-related data (such as heart rate, weight, and injury history) strictly for the purpose of creating customized training plans. By creating an account, users explicitly consent to this processing under GDPR Article 9. We do not sell or share your medical data with third-party advertisers.

2. Data Security & Encryption

We take your privacy seriously. All user passwords and third-party API credentials (e.g., Garmin Connect) are secured using military-grade encryption (bcrypt and AES Fernet algorithms). Endura IQ administrators do not have access to your raw Garmin passwords. All data is securely hosted on MongoDB cloud infrastructure.

3. Third-Party Integrations

Endura IQ connects to Garmin Connect to automatically sync planned and executed workouts. This integration requires your explicit consent and credentials, which are encrypted. You can disconnect this integration at any time by clearing your credentials within your account settings.

4. Right to be Forgotten

You have full control over your data. In the "Account" tab of the application, you can permanently delete your account. This action immediately and irreversibly erases all your workouts, physiological data, messages, and login credentials from our servers.
